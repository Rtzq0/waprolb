Meetups

We congregate monthly to showcase, learn, share, & network. We meet on the 3rd Thursday of every month in Long Beach. RSVP at www.meetup.com/lbtech.

In addition, we gather weekly on Saturday mornings for some __code & coffee,__ a series of meetups hosted by MADE in long beach. It's a public place to gather and work on your own projects while surrounded with other local nerds. It's loose, relaxed, and fun.
